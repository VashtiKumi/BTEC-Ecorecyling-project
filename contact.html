Frequently Asked Questions

üîç
How can I bring EcoRecycle Hub to my school? +

Contact us through the form above or email us directly at info@ecorecyclehub.edu. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your school's needs and create a customized environmental education program. Our team will work with you to integrate our resources into your curriculum.

Are your resources free to use? +

Yes! All our educational resources are completely free for schools and educators. We believe environmental education should be accessible to everyone. This includes lesson plans, worksheets, posters, and digital materials.

Do you offer teacher training? +

We provide comprehensive teacher training workshops, both in-person and virtual, to help educators effectively teach environmental topics. Our training covers curriculum integration, hands-on activities, and assessment strategies.

Can you visit our school for presentations? +

Yes, we offer school visits and assembly presentations. Our team can present to individual classes or entire school assemblies. Contact us to discuss scheduling and availability in your area. We typically book 2-4 weeks in advance.

What age groups do you serve? +

We serve all grade levels from elementary through high school (K-12). Our resources are differentiated by age group, with age-appropriate content and activities for each level.

What are the costs for your services? +

Most of our services are free, including digital resources and basic consultations. Some specialized services like extended on-site training or custom curriculum development may have associated costs, which we'll discuss upfront.
